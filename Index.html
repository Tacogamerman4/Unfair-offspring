About Us

We are a collective of individuals, who, through the most perplexing and unforeseen circumstances, have endeavored to produce compositions of an extremely unconventional and provocative nature. Our pursuit is not only to challenge the norms of musical creation but to fully embrace the absurdity of it all, thus rendering our existence one of great complexity and perplexing joy.
